About Us

Adoptables is where a person can adopt an domesticated animal into their own care. The incentive for creating this website is to prevent further cases of euthanized shelters animals, and help them find homes by creating a centralized website for animal shelters. Users will be able to view the list of of animals waiting to be adopted, and a short description of the pet. Adopters are able to easily get in content with these shelters and arrange for a meeting with their new best friends.
